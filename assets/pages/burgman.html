Suzuki Burgman Street 125 is a scooter available at a starting price of Rs. 89,239 in India. It is available in 2 variants and 10 colours with top variant price starting from Rs. 92,809. The Suzuki Burgman Street 125 is powered by 124cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 8.58 bhp and a torque of 10 Nm. With front disc and rear drum brakes, Suzuki Burgman Street 125 comes up with combined braking system of both wheels. This Burgman Street 125 scooter weighs 110 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 5.5 liters.

Suzuki Burgman Street 125 Colours & Design

The Burgman Street 125 is available in five colours - red, white, grey, white and black. The Burgman Street 125 borrows majority of the big scooter styling from its bigger sibling. This scooter is known for its sharp styling and the presence of large body panels makes it feel extremely aspirational. Plus, the LED lights, large floorboard and seat along with nice flowing lines on the side makes it look quite good.

Suzuki Burgman Street 125 Mileage & Performance

The Burgman Street 125 is based on the Access chassis and driveline, which means peppy performance for a 125 cc. The motor is BS6-compliant and features fuel-injection which is claimed to provide smoother power delivery and better fuel efficiency as compared to the previous carbureted model. The revised engine produces 8.7 bhp and 10 Nm which means the power remains unchanged, but the torque figure has gone down by 0.2 Nm. Another update has come in the form of a new integrated engine start and kill switch with Suzuki Easy Start System. In terms of mileage, the Burgman Street 125 churns out a decent 40 kmpl - 45 kmpl.
